Know right now

BioBloom is a sustainability project aimed at reducing paper waste in educational settings by promoting the use of eco-friendly materials. It focuses on recycling and reusing paper while introducing seed pencils that can grow into plants after use. The initiative is designed for students at the Colegio Bilingüe Nuestra Señora del Rosario in Bogotá, fostering environmental awareness and encouraging sustainable practices among upper-grade students.
