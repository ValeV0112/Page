About BioBloom

BioBloom is an eco-friendly initiative dedicated to reducing school-related pollution by creating sustainable educational materials. Our project combines recycling and reusing paper waste with promoting biodegradable products, such as seed-embedded pencils. Aimed at inspiring students to adopt greener habits, BioBloom encourages an environmentally responsible culture in schools. By providing innovative and practical solutions, we strive to transform traditional school supplies into eco-friendly alternatives, ultimately reducing our environmental impact and fostering sustainability in educational spaces.

Principal ideas

Seed-embedded pencils
Organic erasers
Recycled sheets

BioBloom is a sustainability project aimed at reducing paper waste in educational settings by promoting the use of eco-friendly materials. It focuses on recycling and reusing paper while introducing seed pencils that can grow into plants after use. The initiative is designed for students at the Colegio Bilingüe Nuestra Señora del Rosario in Bogotá, fostering environmental awareness and encouraging sustainable practices among upper-grade students.
